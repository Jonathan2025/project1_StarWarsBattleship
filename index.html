Star Wars Battleship Attack Set!

How to Play the Game

New from Lego Star Wars! The planet is under attack and the faith of the republic is in your hands! This game is a twist on the board game Battleship. once the players start the game, the game pieces will be scattered throughout the grid. Player one must hunt down the droid army by hover over the grid and clicking to strike! At the same time, player two strikes back and attempts to destory player one's game pieces. The player with the most points when the timer stops or the first to reach the maximum points wins! May the force be with you!
